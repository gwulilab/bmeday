About BME Day

The GW BME Departmentwill host its 3rd BME Day: "Innovation and Design" on Monday, November 5th, 2018 at The George Washington University, Science and Engineering Hall, 800 22nd St. NW, Ste 5000, Washington, DC 20052. This event will showcase Faculty and student research projects, highlight the collaborative nature of the Biomedical Engineering field, and raise awareness of biomedical engineering at the George Washington University and the Washington DC region. The day will consist of student poster sessions, a moderated panel discussion on how to land a job within Biomedical Engineering, and selected talks from our faculty and invited guest speakers.
